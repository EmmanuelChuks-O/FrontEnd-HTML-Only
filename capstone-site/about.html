My Creative Journey

Hi, I’m Emmanuel Okoh — a front-end developer with a background in architecture and CAD design. My passion lies in blending aesthetics with functionality, whether it’s designing intuitive web interfaces, photographing urban environments, or sketching technical layouts.

I began my journey in architectural studies, where I developed an eye for detail and structure. Over time, my curiosity for digital experiences led me into front-end development — where I now create modern, responsive websites that tell powerful stories.

What Drives Me

  • 📸 Capturing stories through photography — models, structures, and events like F1 in Montreal 2025.
  • 📐 Bringing clarity to ideas using CAD tools and design thinking.
  • 💻 Turning design visions into responsive and accessible web pages.
  • 🧠 Constantly growing — from HTML basics to full front-end stacks with real-world use.
